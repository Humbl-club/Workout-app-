## Daily "Non-Negotiables" (Mandatory Warm-up)

(10-15 minutes daily)

1.  **Spinal Health (The McGill Big 3):** 2 sets. Descending reps (5, 3, 1). 10s holds.

    *   Bird-Dog
2.  **Thoracic (T-Spine) & Shoulder Mobility:**
    *   Foam Roller T-Spine Extensions (15 reps)
    *   Thread the Needle (Quadruped Rotation) (8 reps/side)
